STORY :

Once upon a time, there was a normal boy who lived a normal life with a normal family. His mother and father were always there for him whenever he needed them. His hobby? Recording videos of his daily life, whether outside, at school or at home. Then one day, on an ordinary morning before school, as he pressed the recording button on his camera, the world around him hushed in an instant. For 30 seconds, the small boy was left in complete silence. The TV he had been watching abruptly turned off. His mother, who had been doing the dishes, and his father, who had been preparing breakfast, all at once, stopped moving. The entire house, which seemed completely abandoned, was only filled with the boy's uneven breathing and a faint knocking sound, which can be likened to his heartbeats, as it got progressively louder and brisk. His camera,still recording, captured everything. "Mom...?" he uttered.
